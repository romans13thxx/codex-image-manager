# Doll Garden Flash Social Video Prompt Pack

Source: user-provided reference set, 2026-05-03.

Use this pack to recreate the visual grammar of a dreamy flower-room portrait series without copying the original person's identity, exact face, UI brand, or screenshots. Generate the clean subject scene only.

## Taxonomy

- topCategory: `real-human`
- subCategory: `home-lifestyle`
- scene: `flower-sunroom`
- skinTone: `light`
- appeal: `cute`
- subject: `female`
- primary ratios: `9:16`, `4:5`, occasional `4:3 landscape`
- output mode: clean image only, no app interface overlay

## Analysis Dimensions

1. Composition:
   - Layered glasshouse framing: window frame, lace curtain, white chair, table edge, umbrella ribs, and flower clusters create overlapping foreground/midground/background planes.
   - The face is usually placed on the right third or center third, with flowers or props balancing the opposite side.
   - Use diagonal arm lines and umbrella ribs to guide the eye to the face.
   - Leave small accidental crops at screen edges only when it helps candid camera realism.
   - Avoid perfectly centered studio symmetry except for the full-body umbrella shot.

2. Background:
   - White sunroom or cafe corner with glass doors, lace curtains, bright garden plants outside, and white wrought-iron furniture.
   - Dense pastel flowers: lavender roses, peach roses, pale blue hydrangea, pink hydrangea, baby's breath, soft greenery.
   - Secondary art props: small framed flower painting, sunflower painting, mirror with ornate white frame, note wall, plush toys, lace tablecloth.

3. Character:
   - Fictional adult woman in her mid-20s; doll-like editorial styling, not a minor and not childlike.
   - Long dusty pink-blonde wavy hair, soft center part, airy volume, glossy strands.
   - Porcelain-soft makeup, glossy large eyes, subtle blush, glossy lips, delicate nose highlight.
   - Slim fashion-doll proportions, graceful neck and hands, but keep anatomy natural.
   - Pale blue chiffon dress with sheer flutter sleeves, white lace camisole edge, asymmetric translucent skirt layer.
   - Silver chain bracelet, pearl bracelet, small pendant necklace; 1-2 key accessories only.

4. Poses:
   - Seated table pose: torso twisted toward camera, one hand at cheek or temple, other hand on chair or lap, gaze upward.
   - Chair/table three-quarter pose: one hand near lips, opposite forearm on lace table, shoulder turned toward flowers.
   - Full-body umbrella pose: standing in front of glass door, transparent floral umbrella held above head, relaxed front-facing stance.
   - Canted close-up umbrella pose: rotated frame, face near lower-right or center, umbrella canopy fills the background.
   - Window/toy counter pose: subject behind glass or table edge, one hand touching hair, plush toys in foreground.

5. Props:
   - Transparent umbrella with white ribs, attached pastel flowers, butterflies, hanging petals.
   - Lace tablecloth, ornate white tabletop mirror, flower baskets, small painted canvas, white wrought-iron chair.
   - Plush toys and paper notes can be used for a cabinet/window variation.

6. Light and color:
   - Direct flash mixed with daylight, deliberately high-key and slightly overexposed.
   - White highlights can bloom, but face and hands must stay readable.
   - Palette: powder blue dress, dusty pink hair, lavender/peach/pale blue flowers, white lace, garden green accents.
   - Add controlled compact-camera texture: slight glare, faint glass reflections, soft vignette, mild compression.

7. Interface cleanup rule:
   - Do not generate any app UI or screenshot overlay.
   - Remove and forbid search bars, play buttons, like/comment/share icons, right-side icon stacks, bottom captions, dates, tags, usernames, QR codes, watermarks, and pseudo interface text.
   - The image should contain only the subject, background, clothing, pose, lighting, and physical props.
