# Prompts

## Master Template

```text
Use the reference set only for style grammar, not identity. Create a new fictional adult woman in {background}. She has {hair}, doll-like but natural adult facial proportions, porcelain-soft makeup, glossy eyes, and wears {dress} with 1-2 delicate silver or pearl accessories. Pose: {pose}. Include {props} as foreground or midground anchors. Build the frame with layered glass, lace, white wrought-iron furniture, pastel flowers, and garden greenery. Camera: {camera}. Lighting: direct compact-camera flash mixed with daylight, high-key slight overexposure, pastel powder blue/dusty pink/lavender/peach/white palette, controlled grain and glass reflections. Clean image only, no app interface overlay.

Anti-drift: avoid generic modern minimalism, avoid random extra text, avoid messy symbols, avoid distorted hands/faces, avoid losing subject identity, avoid copying the reference person's exact face, avoid app UI, avoid search bar, avoid play button, avoid like/comment/share icons, avoid bottom captions, avoid dates, avoid hashtags, avoid usernames, avoid app logos, avoid watermarks, avoid childlike body, avoid excessive accessories, avoid clutter covering the face, keep clear face, expressive pose, 1-2 key accessories only.
```


## 4-Panel Set Prompt

```text
Use the attached reference images only as style, composition, lighting, and prop references. Do not copy the person's identity or exact face. Create one cohesive 2x2 contact sheet containing four separate clean vertical portrait frames of the same new fictional adult woman in her mid-20s. She has long dusty pink-blonde wavy hair, doll-like but natural adult facial proportions, porcelain-soft makeup, glossy eyes, and wears a powder blue chiffon dress with a white lace camisole edge and delicate silver or pearl jewelry.

Panel 1: seated beside a lace-covered flower cafe table, one hand at cheek, dense lavender roses, peach roses, blue hydrangea, ornate white mirror, small flower painting, high-angle candid flash.
Panel 2: full-body standing in a white glasshouse doorway, holding a transparent umbrella with pastel flowers and butterfly decals, lace curtain and garden plants behind.
Panel 3: canted close-up under the transparent floral umbrella, face near center-right, umbrella ribs and opalescent plastic glints framing the face.
Panel 4: behind a cafe window or glass counter with plush toys in the foreground, note wall and framed sunflower painting behind, one hand touching hair.

Each panel has high-key direct compact-camera flash mixed with daylight, slight overexposure, glass reflections, pastel powder blue, dusty pink, lavender, peach, white lace, and garden green accents. Clean subject images only. Do not add any app interface, search bar, play triangle, side icons, like/comment/share icons, bottom captions, dates, hashtags, usernames, QR codes, logos, watermarks, or pseudo UI text.

Avoid distorted hands/faces, extra fingers, copied likeness, childlike body, oversexualized styling, random readable text, app UI, interface marks, clutter covering the face, muddy colors, dark horror lighting.
```
