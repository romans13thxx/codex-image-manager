# Style Guide

## Visual Formula

```text
flower sunroom + lace/glass layers + pastel flower density + powder blue chiffon dress + dusty pink-blonde hair + direct compact flash + clean image with no app UI
```

## Composition Rules

- Use foreground occlusion intentionally: lace table edge, glass reflection, umbrella ribs, plush toys, or flower stems.
- Keep the face readable and unobstructed; the face is the anchor.
- Place flowers on the opposite third from the face to balance the frame.
- Use one dominant diagonal: forearm, umbrella rib, table edge, or window frame.
- For vertical 9:16, allow slight edge crops to create social screenshot realism.
- For full-body images, center the subject and use the umbrella canopy as a halo-like frame.

## Background Kit

- White sunroom, glass door, lace curtain, garden greenery, white wrought-iron chair.
- Lace tablecloth, ornate white mirror, flower baskets, small flower painting, sunflower canvas.
- Pastel flowers: lavender roses, peach roses, pale blue hydrangea, pink hydrangea, baby's breath.
- Optional cabinet scene: note wall, plush toys, glass reflection.

## Character Kit

- Fictional adult woman in her mid-20s.
- Long dusty pink-blonde wavy hair, soft center part, airy volume.
- Doll-like but natural adult facial proportions, glossy eyes, soft blush, glossy lips.
- Powder blue chiffon dress, sheer flutter sleeves, white lace camisole edge, asymmetric translucent skirt.
- Silver bracelet, pearl bracelet, small pendant necklace; keep only 1-2 accessories visible.

## Lighting

- Direct flash plus daylight, high-key, slight overexposure.
- Whites may bloom, but eyes, lips, hands, and dress edges must stay detailed.
- Add faint glass glare, soft vignette, and mild compression only.

## Interface Cleanup

- Do not generate any app interface.
- Forbid search bar, play button, like/comment/share icons, right-side icon stack, bottom captions, dates, hashtags, usernames, QR codes, app logos, watermarks, and pseudo UI text.
- The subject image should contain only the adult character, physical props, clothing, environment, lighting, and natural camera artifacts.

## Negative Constraints

```text
avoid generic modern minimalism, avoid random extra text, avoid messy symbols,
avoid distorted hands/faces, avoid losing subject identity,
avoid copying the reference person's exact face, avoid app UI, avoid search bar,
avoid play button, avoid like/comment/share icons, avoid bottom captions,
avoid dates, avoid hashtags, avoid usernames, avoid app logos, avoid watermarks,
avoid childlike body, avoid excessive accessories, avoid clutter covering the face,
avoid oversexualized styling, avoid muddy greens, avoid dark horror lighting
```
