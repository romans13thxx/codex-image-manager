# Lessons

## Stable Rules

- The look depends on layered depth: glass reflection + lace + flowers + white furniture. Without those layers it becomes a generic pastel portrait.
- The face must stay readable even when flash bloom and foreground props are used.
- Transparent umbrella works best as a framing device, not as a large object covering the subject.
- Jewelry should stay minimal. Too many small accessories compete with flowers and UI.

## Risks To Watch

- Strong "doll" wording can push the output into toy or childlike anatomy. Always pair it with `fictional adult woman in her mid-20s` and `natural adult facial proportions`.
- Any UI wording can create unwanted app artifacts. Keep UI completely forbidden for this project.
- Dense flowers can hide the face or hands. Specify face unobstructed and 1-2 foreground props.
