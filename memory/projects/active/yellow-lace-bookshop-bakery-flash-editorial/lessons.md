# Lessons

## 2026-05-03

- This set is strongest when treated as a location-and-pose system, not only as a yellow outfit.
- The repeatable anchors are yellow glossy lace dress, rose-blonde hair, white platform knee boots, silver chain bag, pearl/silver jewelry, compact-camera flash, and one strong scene prop per frame.
- The best backgrounds use visible structure: bookshelves plus mirror, red bakery window grid, black lamppost, iron cafe gate, brick doorway.
- UI should remain optional. The search pill, bottom-left yellow tags, and play triangle are useful only for screenshot variants.
- Age drift is a major risk. Always specify adult 22+ and avoid teen-coded language.
- Text drift is a major risk. Use pseudo menu marks and no readable book titles unless an exact text target is explicitly requested.
- Outfit drift is a major risk. Keep the dress opaque and fashion-editorial, not lingerie or bedroom-coded.
