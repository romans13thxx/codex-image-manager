# yellow-lace bookshop bakery flash editorial style guide

> Project-level style rules. This is a reusable visual system, not a request to copy a specific face, real person, platform watermark, or exact UI screenshot.

## Visual Thesis

- Adult female fashion model, clearly 22+, fictional identity.
- American spicy-girl / gyaru-adjacent street snap energy, but polished as fashion editorial.
- Yellow glossy lace-trim mini dress with opaque lining, rose-blonde loose waves, white platform knee boots, silver mini chain bag, pearl and silver jewelry.
- Compact-camera direct flash over real city textures: bookshelves, red bakery window frames, brick walls, black lampposts, iron cafe gates, sidewalk trees.
- The image should feel like a social app capture from a fashion outing, while remaining clean enough for production reuse.

## Selected Style Logic

- Base photo layer: `P007` disposable camera flash photo.
- Optional stronger flash layer: `P008` paparazzi tabloid flash, used lightly for hard foreground highlights.
- Fashion layer: `F001` cyber gyaru street snap plus `F010` 1990s Harajuku street snap magazine, restrained to avoid accessory clutter.
- Space layer: `S002` Showa-era kissaten cafe interior for warm cafe/bakery interiors, adapted to modern generic street cafe scenes.
- Optional interface layer: `G008` Y2K techno magazine layout or soft app-screenshot chrome, only when screenshot mode is requested.

## Composition Principles

- Vertical phone-first framing, 4:5 or 3:4 crop. Subject occupies about 65-80% of image height.
- Face sits near the upper third or close to the central vertical axis; eyes stay clear and well lit.
- Use strong background geometry: bookshelf rows, mirror seam, red window mullions, brick courses, black lamppost, cafe railing, doorway frame.
- Body creates a diagonal or S-curve: tilted shoulders, hip shift, one knee raised, one boot kicked backward, one arm over hair.
- Anchor every frame to one prop: book, lamppost, railing, silver bag, bakery bench, mirror, window grid.
- Keep the environment legible. The scene must read as bookshop/library, bakery window, brick cafe doorway, or street pole, not generic studio.

## Background System

- Bookshop/library: dark wood shelves, dense book spines, mirror reflection doubling the subject, warm hanging lamp, narrow aisle.
- Bakery window: deep red painted window grid, hand-written white menu marks as pseudo text, warm bulb, indoor tables and pastry counter behind glass.
- Street pole: black glossy lamppost, brick residential/cafe facade, air-conditioner units, railings, road edge, realistic urban texture.
- Cafe gate: black iron gate, white outdoor chair/table, leafy street background, sidewalk tile, awning edge.
- Brick doorway: red-brown brick wall, white door trim, concrete floor, high white cafe table, small ground lights.

## Person Design

- Adult female model, 22+, fictional non-celebrity face.
- Slender fashion-doll-inspired proportions with natural adult anatomy, long legs, narrow waist, soft shoulders.
- Face: large clear eyes, glossy lips, soft blush, pale shimmer makeup, calm direct gaze or soft half-smile.
- Hair: long rose-blonde or pale peach-blonde loose waves, center or soft off-center part, high volume around the crown.
- Avoid childlike proportions, teen-coded styling, celebrity likeness, exact face copying, over-smoothed plastic skin, broken hands.

## Outfit And Props

- Dress: bright lemon-yellow glossy lace-trim mini dress, opaque lining, thin straps, small rhinestone details, lace hem.
- Shoes: white platform knee boots, chunky sole, clean silhouette.
- Bag: small silver metallic chain bag, structured flap or small shoulder purse.
- Jewelry: pearl choker, layered delicate necklaces, silver bracelet stack, black bead bracelet, rings, long manicured nails.
- Props: open book, red hardcover book, bakery bench, lamppost, cafe gate, mirror, window glass, white cafe table/chair.
- Limit each frame to 1-2 active props so hands and accessories do not merge.

## Pose Library

- Bookshop open-book turn: torso angled to shelves, one hand holding an open book, head turned back to camera, hair over one shoulder.
- Bookshop red-book front: one red hardcover book held low at waist, other hand near hair or bookshelf, mirror reflection behind.
- Bakery hand-on-hair stand: one arm lifted with hand in hair, other arm relaxed, one hip pushed, window grid centered behind.
- Bakery bench boot-kick: standing near bench, one knee bent backward so the white boot crosses foreground, silver bag hanging from both hands.
- Street lamppost kick: one hand holds lamppost, one leg lifted backward, body leaning away from pole, face tilted toward lens.
- Cafe gate lean: one hand on gate or rail, one knee raised, opposite hand near cheek, soft S-curve.
- Brick doorway kneel: low kneeling pose, one hand on hair, one hand presenting the silver chain bag, boot sole visible but not oversized.

## Camera And Light

- Compact-camera or phone flash realism, 24-35mm equivalent for full body, 35-50mm for medium portraits.
- Direct on-camera flash on face, hair, dress, and boots; keep crisp specular highlights on yellow fabric.
- Ambient light varies by scene: warm lamp in bookshop, bakery interior bulb, hard street daylight, shaded cafe doorway.
- Slight overexposure on dress is acceptable; face and hands must stay readable.
- Keep texture visible: lace hem, glossy fabric, wood grain, brick mortar, glass reflections, scuffed black metal.

## Optional Interface Layer

Default output should have no UI, watermark, search overlay, or hashtags.

Use screenshot mode only when explicitly requested:

- Semi-transparent rounded search capsule near top-left or top-center.
- Search text can be generic Chinese such as `美式辣妹穿搭`.
- Small bottom-left yellow hashtag text may appear as pseudo UI.
- Optional centered translucent play triangle if imitating a short-video still.
- UI must not cover the face, hands, dress details, or active prop.

## Fail Conditions

- Looks underage or teen-coded.
- Copies a real face or creates celebrity resemblance.
- Outfit becomes lingerie-like, bedroom-coded, or transparent.
- Hands, fingers, jewelry, bag chain, books, boot sole, or railing become fused or deformed.
- Background loses the scene identity and becomes a generic blank wall.
- Random readable brand names, exact bakery menu text, watermarks, or platform UI appear when clean mode is requested.
- Flash becomes soft studio glamour instead of compact-camera flash over real location texture.

## Negative Constraints

avoid generic modern minimalism, avoid random extra text, avoid messy symbols, avoid distorted hands/faces, avoid losing subject identity, no childlike or teen-coded appearance, no celebrity likeness, no real brand logo, no readable store name, no watermark, no search UI unless screenshot mode is requested, no hashtags unless screenshot mode is requested, no transparent dress, no lingerie framing, no bedroom setting, no plastic skin, no extra fingers, no extra limbs, no warped books, no deformed boots, no fused bag chain
