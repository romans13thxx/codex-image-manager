# sunlit-glass-corridor-editorial style guide

> Project-level style rules. If these conflict with general `real-human` preferences, this project guide wins.

## Must Keep

- Adult female model, clearly 22+; fashion editorial, not teen-coded.
- Modern public interior: glass corridor, campus lobby, library atrium, museum passage, or station-like glass hall.
- Floor-to-ceiling windows with black mullions, glossy floor, visible outdoor greenery, road, grass, hill, or white campus building.
- Hard afternoon sunlight through glass, high bloom, overexposed rim light, long rectangular shadow blocks.
- Long rose-gold or peach-blonde hair, gentle glossy makeup, realistic adult face and hands.
- Ivory fitted knit top, beige plaid pleated fashion skirt, black belt, white socks, chunky black platform loafers or Mary Janes.
- White quilted backpack or shoulder bag; stacked bracelets and small necklace.
- Poses should feel like a continuous fashion-photo set: vending block lean, sun stretch, walking spin, window crouch, window touch turn.

## Composition Logic

- The background grid is the backbone: window frames and ceiling beams should lead the eye to the face.
- Use big light/shadow shapes: sun rectangles on the wall, shadow silhouettes, and glossy floor reflections.
- Subject should not be dead center; prefer lower-right/lower-middle third with upper window negative space.
- Body lines should echo the architecture: bent knee, crossed arms, stretched leg, turned torso, flowing hair, or bag swing.
- Anchor each image to one prop: white vending block, window rail, quilted bag, backpack, latch, yellow panel, or floor outlet.
- Keep phone-screenshot vertical framing, but leave enough headroom so the UI overlay never covers the face.

## Background Rules

- Use real public-space cues: window handles, black rails, floor tile seams, ceiling slats, small service fixtures, vending-machine edges.
- Exterior should stay bright and soft: trees, grass, road, hill, white building, blue sky.
- Palette should balance aqua glass and green exterior with warm sun and beige outfit.
- Include yellow architectural panels in some frames, but do not turn the scene into a shopping mall or studio set.

## Interface Layer

- Default production mode: no readable UI, no watermark, no platform names.
- Screenshot mode: faint top-left frosted search capsule with magnifier icon; small bottom-left tag/caption stub cropped by the frame.
- UI layer must stay low opacity, never cover face, hands, shoes, or key prop.

## Fail Conditions

- Looks like a minor, schoolgirl, cosplay uniform, or idol fan screenshot.
- Readable brand/platform UI, watermark, username, hashtags, or long fake Chinese text appears.
- Hands, bracelets, shoes, plaid skirt, or window mullions are deformed.
- Lighting becomes flat studio light instead of hard sun through glass.
- Background loses the glass-corridor identity.
- Outfit drifts away from ivory knit top, beige plaid skirt, black chunky shoes, and white bag system.
