# real-human / outdoor-street: blue parking flash doll editorial

> Reusable style module. Use for adult fashion-photo sets with a bright outdoor retail-parking backdrop, hard sun, direct flash, and playful pose continuity. This is a reusable reference pattern, not a request to copy any real brand, watermark, UI overlay, or face.

## Core Look

- Category: `real-human`
- Subcategory: `outdoor-street`
- Scene axis: `outdoor`
- Skin tone axis: `light`
- Appeal axis: `cute`
- Subject axis: `female`
- Base style: point-and-shoot flash fashion editorial, sunlit retail parking lot, casual paparazzi snapshot energy
- Supporting style: blue/yellow wayfinding colors, sun-bleached concrete, metal canopy shadows, dollcore fashion styling
- Format: vertical phone editorial series, mixed full-body, seated, cart, selfie, and close portrait frames

## Composition Principles

- Use strong environmental geometry: painted blue parking floor, white lane arrows, metal canopy beams, curb edges, striped poles, shopping cart rails.
- Keep the model near a vertical anchor: pole, column, cart handle, curb edge, or phone held close to lens.
- Use diagonal tension: high-angle camera, tilted shoulders, S-curve torso, legs crossing into the foreground, canopy beams leading toward the subject.
- Maintain large real-location background signals: parked cars, low shrubs, generic retail wall, loading bay, shopping carts, warning stripes.
- Put the subject between hard sunlight and fill flash: bright face and outfit, crisp shadows on ground, occasional backlight flare.
- Use blue/yellow contrast as the main palette, with warm skin and pale peach-blonde hair as the soft counterweight.
- Leave a candid screenshot feeling, but do not include actual UI overlays, hashtags, search pills, watermarks, or readable social app text by default.

## Person Design

- Adult female model, clearly 22+; non-minor, fashion editorial styling.
- Slender fashion-doll-inspired proportions, long legs, narrow waist, soft shoulders; natural adult anatomy.
- Face: large clear eyes, glossy lips, soft blush, pale shimmer makeup, clean lashes, neutral-to-curious expression.
- Hair: long peach-blonde or rose-gold loose waves, center or soft off-center part, light wind movement.
- Avoid: childlike framing, teen-coded wording, celebrity resemblance, identical face from references, plastic skin, broken hands.

## Outfit And Props

- Outfit: dusty-blue ribbed strapless tube top or cropped denim-knit bandeau, beige low-rise utility shorts, pale blue belt.
- Shoes: chunky white or silver sneakers, slouchy white leg warmers or thick socks.
- Accessories: stacked bracelets, black bead bracelet, silver charms, rings, tiny star/cross necklace, small hair clip.
- Props: shopping cart, newspaper or folded magazine as sun shade, smartphone for selfie close-up, striped parking pole, curb/bench edge.
- Background props: generic retail sign with abstract yellow blocks, shopping carts, parked cars, planters, loading truck, canopy frame.

## Pose Library

- Pole lean: one arm overhead across hair, one hand at wrist, torso in S-curve, face angled toward camera.
- Salute lean: one hand shields forehead from sun, other hand reaches to a pole, hip angled, one knee forward.
- Curb sit: knees pulled together toward camera, arms folded over knees, chunky shoes large in foreground.
- Cart recline: seated inside shopping cart, legs extended toward camera, one hand holding newspaper above head.
- Selfie wink: phone close to lens in one hand, other hand near cheek, wink or half-smile, tight crop.
- Kneeling street pose: one knee down, one leg bent, hand at hair or hip, body leaning away from a striped pole.
- Backlit canopy kneel: kneeling on newspaper, sun flare behind canopy beams, one hand on waist, calm direct gaze.

## Camera And Light

- Phone or compact-camera look, 24-35mm equivalent for full-body shots, 35-50mm equivalent for close portraits.
- Slight high angle for standing and cart shots; lower frontal angle for curb sit.
- Direct fill flash on face and torso; hard midday sun from upper side or back; crisp shadows and small lens flare.
- Keep texture visible: cracked blue paint, rough concrete, metal rails, scuffed poles, cart wire grid.

## Optional Interface Layer

Only use this when the output is intentionally a social screenshot mockup:

- Semi-transparent search pill in the top-left corner.
- Minimal back arrow or app chrome.
- Small yellow hashtag text near the bottom-left.

Default production output should disable this layer.
