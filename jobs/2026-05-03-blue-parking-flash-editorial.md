## Selection Criteria

- Adult appearance is clear.
- No real logo, no readable store text, no watermark, no UI overlay.
- Blue parking/service area remains recognizable.
- Direct flash and hard sun are both visible.
- Hands, shoes, cart wires, and body anatomy are usable.
- Outfit and accessories stay consistent across the set.

## Failure Fallback

- If faces or hands distort, regenerate with tighter prompt: "clean face, natural hands, five fingers per hand, bracelets do not merge with fingers."
- If brand text appears, regenerate with: "abstract yellow block shapes only, no letters, no readable signage."
- If output becomes too glamour/studio, increase: "weathered concrete, parking lane arrows, shopping cart bay, compact-camera snapshot."

## Memory Candidates

- `blue parking paint + black-yellow pole + hard sun + fill flash` as a reusable outdoor-street anchor.
- `shopping cart / newspaper / phone selfie` as a reusable prop trio for candid fashion sets.
